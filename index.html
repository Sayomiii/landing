Entrepreneurship

I started an accessory business in 2019 to promote uniqueness in fashion. My participation in the 2022 Hult Prize competition enlightened me on the importance of sustainable fashion, which inspired me to incorporate SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production) and SDG 13 (Climate Action) into my business practices. Currently, we promote sustainability by posting weekly on the importance of having a unique style. This encourages our audience to value their fashion choices more and reduce unnecessary disposal of clothes and fashion items. By advocating for unique styles, we aim to combat the negative impacts of fast fashion. We are committed to taking even bigger steps towards sustainability in the coming years.
